About Quaver

Quaver is the ultimate community-driven and open-source competitive rhythm game. We're aiming to take the concept of VSRGs to a new level by bringing competition and e-sports into the mix.

Although the game may seem difficult at first glance, you don't have to be a pro to play Quaver! This game is for everyone - casual and seasoned. We have a community of developers from all over the globe contributing to the open-source game, so join in on the fun!
